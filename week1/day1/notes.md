# Day 1 - Introduction to Salesforce
## What is Salesforce?
Today I learned that Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM (Customer Relationship Management) platform. In simple terms, it helps companies store customer information, track their interactions, and manage things like sales, support, and marketing all in one place.
## Why Salesforce is used?
From what I understood, companies use Salesforce to:
- Manage customer relationships better  
- Automate their daily business tasks  
- Improve sales and customer service  
- Keep all customer data organized and easy to access  
## Key Concepts I learned
- **CRM:** A system used to manage customer details and interactions  
- **Cloud Computing:** Data is stored online instead of on a personal computer  
- **SaaS:** Software that can be used through the internet without installing it  
## Real-world understanding
I realized that many companies depend on Salesforce to manage their customers, track leads, and handle support requests. It helps teams stay organized and work more efficiently.
## What I learned today
- Salesforce is widely used across many industries  
- It is completely cloud-based  
- CRM is very important for managing business growth
